Explanation and Instructions

The Word Frequency Counter is a user-friendly tool that allows you to analyze the frequency of words in any text. It not only counts how often each word appears but also displays the total word count and the percentage that each word contributes to the total. The results are shown in a clear table format, making it easy to understand the distribution of words in your text.

How to Use the Word Frequency Counter

  1. Enter Your Text:

    • At the top of the page, you’ll see a large text area labeled with “Enter your text here…”.
    • Click inside this text area and type or paste the text you want to analyze.

  2. Count Word Frequency:

    • After entering your text, click the “Count Word Frequency” button just below the text area.
    • The tool will instantly process your input and display the results.

  3. View the Results:

    • Total Words: Just below the button, you’ll see the total word count for your text.
    • Word Frequency Table: Below the total word count, you’ll find a table that lists each word, how many times it appears, and the percentage of the total word count that it represents.

Interpreting the Results

  • Word: The actual word from your text.
  • Frequency: The number of times the word appears in the text.
  • Percentage: The percentage that this word makes up of the total word count, helping you identify the most common words in your text.

Example

Suppose you enter the following text:

This is a simple example. 
This tool will count word frequency 
and display the results in a table.

After clicking “Count Word Frequency,” the table will show:

  • Total Words: 18
  • Word Frequency Table:
    WordFrequencyPercentage
    this211%
    a211%
    is16%
    simple16%
    example16%
    tool16%
    will16%
    count16%
    word16%
    frequency16%
    and16%
    display16%
    the16%
    results16%
    in16%
    table16%

This output shows that the word “this” and “a” appears twice, accounting for 11% of the total word count, while each of the other words appears once, making up 6%.

Ready to Analyze?

Simply type or paste your text into the provided area and click the “Count Word Frequency” button to get started. Your results will be displayed immediately, helping you quickly understand the word distribution in your content.
